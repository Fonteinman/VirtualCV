Introduction

Hello! My name is Andre Croukamp. I'm an advisor with a passion for revenue and reporting. Welcome to my "About Me" page where you can learn more about my background, interests.

Background

My career started off in accounting after finishing my studies at the NWU in 2010. I manoeuvred my way through articles by servicing private sector and public sector clients. I managed to stay in the consulting space after my articles by joining the 13th ranked globally auditing firm Kreston South Africa as a consulting manager.

My passion for reporting was put to the test and after long hours and hard work I made my way to the number one Municipal Financial Management Software providers namely Munsoft. In the Advisory office I worked my way up to be appointed as Head of Revenue and Reporting.

I specialize in local government statutory reporting, and I have worked on projects involving planning, design, building and implementing of a fully integrated reporting module used by a quarter of the municipalities in South Africa.
